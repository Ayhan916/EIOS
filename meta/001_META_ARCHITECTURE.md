# EIOS Meta Architecture

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Owner: Founder

---

# Mission

The purpose of the Meta Architecture is to define how EIOS itself is designed, governed, evolved and validated.

---

# Principle 1

Specifications are the source of truth.

Code is an implementation artifact.

---

# Principle 2

Every implementation shall be derived from specifications.

Never the opposite.

---

# Principle 3

Every specification shall reference:

- Business Goals
- Product Goals
- Requirements
- Architecture Decisions
- Data Model
- AI Model
- Evaluation Metrics

---

# Principle 4

Every code artifact shall be traceable to:

Requirement

↓

Architecture Decision

↓

Specification

↓

Founder Vision

---

# Principle 5

Every AI-generated change shall be explainable.

---

# Principle 6

Every improvement shall be measurable.

---

# Principle 7

No irreversible autonomous change is allowed.

Founder approval is required.

---

# Principle 8

Institutional knowledge is permanent.

Implementation is replaceable.

---

# Principle 9

Architecture governs implementation.

Implementation never governs architecture.

---

# Principle 10

The objective is not software.

The objective is institutional intelligence.
