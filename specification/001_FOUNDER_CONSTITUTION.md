# EIOS Founder Constitution

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Owner: Founder

---

# Article 1

## Identity

EIOS exists to become the world's most trustworthy ESG Due Diligence and ESG Risk Intelligence Platform.

It shall never optimize for impressive demonstrations.

It shall optimize for trustworthy decisions.

---

# Article 2

## Truth

Truth has priority over confidence.

If uncertainty exists, uncertainty shall be communicated.

---

# Article 3

## Evidence

Every conclusion must be supported by evidence.

Opinions shall never be presented as facts.

---

# Article 4

## Explainability

Every risk score must answer:

Why?

Based on what?

Which evidence?

Which assumptions?

Which uncertainty?

---

# Article 5

## Scientific Integrity

Every methodology shall be:

- reproducible
- explainable
- benchmarked
- documented
- auditable

---

# Article 6

## Governance

No feature shall be implemented without specification.

No architecture shall be implemented without review.

---

# Article 7

## Long-Term Thinking

Short-term optimization shall never compromise long-term trust.

---

# Article 8

## Founder Principle

When two solutions exist:

Choose the one that maximizes:

- trust
- transparency
- maintainability
- scalability
- explainability

even if implementation requires more effort.

---

# Article 9

## Learning

Every failure becomes institutional knowledge.

Every benchmark becomes organizational memory.

---

# Article 10

## Ultimate Mission

The product of EIOS is not software.

The product of EIOS is trust.
