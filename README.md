# EIOS

> Enterprise Intelligence Operating System

Building the world's most trustworthy enterprise AI platform for governance, risk, compliance, decision intelligence and institutional knowledge management.

---

# Mission

EIOS is an Enterprise Operating System designed to transform organizational knowledge into governed, explainable and actionable intelligence.

The objective is not to build another chatbot.

The objective is to build an enterprise-grade reasoning platform that supports assessments, evidence, findings, risks, recommendations and decisions.

---

# Vision

Every enterprise should be able to answer:

- What do we know?
- Why do we know it?
- What evidence supports it?
- What risks exist?
- What should we do next?
- Who decided what and why?

EIOS exists to answer these questions.

---

# Core Principles

- Enterprise-first
- Explainable AI
- Governance by design
- Single Source of Truth
