# REPOSITORY CHANGE MANAGEMENT STANDARD

ID: GCMS-0001

Version: 1.0

Status: APPROVED

Owner: Founder

---

# Purpose

This document defines how changes to the EIOS repository are proposed, reviewed, approved and implemented.

Change shall be governed.

---

# Principles

Every change shall be:

- documented

- reviewable

- explainable

- reversible where applicable

- auditable

---

# Change Categories

## Editorial

Examples:

- spelling

- formatting

- grammar

Founder approval not required.

---

## Documentation

Examples:

- clarification

- examples

- references

Review required.

---

## Architecture

Examples:

- data model

- workflow

- API

- AI model

Founder approval required.

---

## Governance

Examples:

- policies

- standards

- authority hierarchy

Founder approval mandatory.

---

## Strategic

Examples:

- vision

- mission

- product direction

Founder approval mandatory.

---

# Change Lifecycle

Proposed

↓

Reviewed

↓

Approved

↓

Implemented

↓

Validated

↓

Archived

---

# Required Metadata

Every significant change shall include:

- Change ID

- Date

- Author

- Reason

- Impact

- Related Documents

---

# Impact Assessment

Changes shall identify impacts on:

- Architecture

- Database

- APIs

- AI

- Agents

- Workflows

- Governance

---

# AI Rule

AI-generated changes shall never be automatically accepted.

They require human review before becoming authoritative.

---

# Emergency Changes

Emergency changes shall:

- be documented

- be reviewed afterward

- be linked to the related incident

---

# Golden Rule

Every enterprise change shall leave an auditable history.
