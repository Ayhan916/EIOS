# ENTERPRISE TERMINOLOGY STANDARD

ID: GTERM-0001

Version: 1.0

Status: APPROVED

Owner: Founder

---

# Purpose

This document defines the canonical terminology of EIOS.

Every enterprise artifact, AI agent, API, workflow and report shall use these definitions consistently.

No document may redefine these terms.

---

# Principles

Terminology shall be:

* canonical

* unambiguous

* explainable

* reusable

* machine-readable

---

# Assessment

Definition:

A structured evaluation process that analyzes one or more enterprise objects against defined criteria.

Produces:

* Findings

* Risks

* Opportunities

* Recommendations

---

# Evidence

Definition:

A verifiable piece of information supporting or contradicting a finding.

Evidence may originate from:

* Documents

* Databases

* APIs

* Human input

* Trusted external sources

Evidence itself is not a conclusion.

---

# Finding

Definition:

An observation derived from analyzing available evidence.

A finding describes what has been identified without assigning business significance.

Findings may support one or more risks or opportunities.

---

# Risk

Definition:

A potential event or condition that may negatively affect objectives.

Every risk shall:

* be classified

* have supporting evidence

* have likelihood

* have impact

* have confidence

---

# Opportunity

Definition:

A potential event or condition that may positively affect objectives.

Every opportunity shall be evaluated using structured criteria.

---

# Recommendation

Definition:

A proposed action intended to mitigate a risk or realize an opportunity.

Recommendations are generated from analysis.

They are not decisions.

---

# Decision

Definition:

A formally approved choice made by an authorized actor based on available information.

A decision may accept, reject or modify recommendations.

---

# Source

Definition:

The origin from which evidence is obtained.

Sources may be:

* internal

* external

* structured

* unstructured

---

# Confidence Score

Definition:

A quantitative estimation of confidence in a finding, risk or recommendation.

Confidence is not correctness.

---

# Trace ID

Definition:

A unique identifier allowing reconstruction of the complete reasoning and processing chain.

---

# Enterprise Memory

Definition:

The governed persistent knowledge repository of EIOS.

Enterprise Memory stores knowledge.

Agents consume and contribute to it.

---

# AI Agent

Definition:

A specialized enterprise capability with defined responsibilities, permissions and outputs.

An agent does not own enterprise knowledge.

---

# Workflow

Definition:

A governed sequence of activities producing enterprise outcomes.

Agents execute workflows.

---

# Governance

Definition:

The system of rules defining authority, control and accountability across EIOS.

---

# Golden Rule

One concept.

One meaning.

One canonical definition.
