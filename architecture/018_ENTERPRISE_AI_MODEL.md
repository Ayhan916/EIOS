ID: AAI-0001

Version: 1.0

# Purpose

The Enterprise AI Model defines how Artificial Intelligence operates inside EIOS.

AI is an enterprise capability.

AI shall never replace governance.

AI shall augment enterprise intelligence.

# Model Independence

EIOS shall not depend on a single LLM.

Supported model providers may include:

- OpenAI

- Anthropic

- Google

- Open Source Models

The orchestration layer shall abstract model providers.

---

# AI Safety

AI shall never:

- invent evidence

- fabricate sources

- bypass permissions

- modify governance decisions

---

# Explainability

Every AI output shall include:

- Reasoning Summary

- Evidence References

- Confidence Score

- Model Version

- Prompt Version

---

# Golden Rule

AI generates recommendations.

Enterprise governance makes decisions.
