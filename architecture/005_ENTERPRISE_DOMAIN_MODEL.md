ID: ADOM-0001

Version: 1.0

# Purpose

The Enterprise Domain Model defines the business universe of EIOS.

Everything inside the platform belongs to a domain.

Every domain contains entities.

Every entity contains relationships.

Every relationship is explicitly defined.
